Summer Program

Eight weeks of Montessori, outdoors.

A hands-on summer program woven with farm, STEAM, and the Montessori peace curriculum.

The Rhythm

Monday – Thursday · 9 a.m. – 2 p.m.

Students are welcome to attend on a weekly basis or enroll for the entire eight-week program. Day rates and half-day programs are also available.

  • Open to Primary (3–6) and Elementary (kindergarten grads and up) students
  • Register by the week or for the full eight weeks
  • Half-day and full-day options
  • Topics introduced through hands-on Montessori lessons
  • Montessori peace curriculum woven through each class
  • S.T.E.A.M. enrichment built into the week

Drop photo: summer-program.jpg

A week might include

Real work, real play, real learning.

Garden & Greenhouse

Planting, harvesting, composting, and tending beds alongside the farm team.

Farm Friends

Daily care routines with our farm animals — responsibility that builds real confidence.

S.T.E.A.M. Studio

Science, engineering, art, and maker projects in dedicated studio sessions.

Peace Curriculum

Mindfulness, grace & courtesy, and global citizenship — a Montessori hallmark.
