Welcome

A diverse community, built on the Montessori philosophy.

Children's Tribe fosters curiosity, creativity, and critical thinking in every student. Our program is based on children's developmental needs and creates freedom within limits through a carefully prepared environment.

Guides follow each student's individual path toward personal academic excellence — raising life-long learners who respect themselves, others, and their environment.

The Farm Curriculum

13 acres. Real animals. Real work. Real stewardship.

Our campus is a working farm. Students get hands-on horticulture in the on-site greenhouse and garden areas, care for a variety of farm animals, and learn the rhythms of seasons through daily chores and long-term projects.

Together, students run the school's Farmers Market — and along the way, many create their own small business to take to market. It's economics, biology, citizenship, and grace-and-courtesy in one beautiful package.
