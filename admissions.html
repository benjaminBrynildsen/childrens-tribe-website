Admissions

Join the tribe.

We welcome families who love their children fiercely and believe in the dignity of childhood.

The Process

Four simple steps.

  1. 1

    Schedule a Tour

    Come walk the campus, meet a guide, and see classrooms in action.

  2. 2

    Submit an Application

    Complete the application and include the assessment fee ($175 per child for grades 1–8).

  3. 3

    Child Visit & Conversation

    Your child spends time in the classroom; we meet with you to discuss fit.

  4. 4

    Enrollment

    Receive an enrollment decision and welcome packet to begin your family's journey.
